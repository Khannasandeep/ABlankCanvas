About the Artist

Hi, I’m Disha, the artist behind these creations!

Painting is something I truly enjoy, and I love making the most of my free time to bring my ideas to life on the canvas. At home, you’ll usually find me curled up with a good book, enjoying a movie, or getting my hands covered in paint. I also love decorating my space—curating rooms that feel like an extension of myself and my art. And no matter where I go, I always return to my bed, wrapped in four cozy layers, for the perfect night’s rest.

I’m a huge dog lover (they’re my absolute favorite, even more than people!).

When I’m not painting, you can bet I’m cheering for cricket or diving into the magical worlds of Harry Potter and The Lord of the Rings.

Fun Fact: I’ve never watched cartoons—so all my creativity comes from the pages of fictional books.

Thanks so much for visiting my gallery! I hope my art brings as much joy and warmth to your life as it does to mine when I create it.
